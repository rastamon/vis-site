Synchronization Software

Through out talented team of programmers and developers, the VIS Team is able to bring you the best acoustic features. We have created a software called Synchronization Notified Callback (SyNC) that leverages spatial audio and machine learning to put you right in the sound of the beat. Whether in the studio, at home, or in your car, you will feel like you are right in the heart of the sound.

SyNC is also able to harmonize the melodies of various instruments to create a combined experience. Our software can even synchronize the sound instruments from different families across the interface!
